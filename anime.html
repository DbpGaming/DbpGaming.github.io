Anime

EP/S means how far i've watched, none means completed.

Sakurasou

i don't know how to explain this anime other than, a ultimate inspirational slice of life.

Neon Genesis Evangelon

cool "mech battle" anime, that slowly morphs into one of the most inspirational animes possible, honestly it's absolutely amazing 10/10

Dragon Ball

The anime adaptation of Akira Toriyama's dragon ball is great, it takes everything about the manga, and adapts it perfectly into the anime format, while providing reasonable filler, that only serves to flesh out the world instead of waste your time.

Fullmetal Alchemist (2003)

FMA03 faithfully adapts the original manga until a certain point, it entirely changes the ending, as the anime was finished way before the manga, but overall its great, watch it before brotherhood, if you're watching both animes as brotherhood ruins certain points of the story that are best spoiler free, i recommend watching FMA03 and then reading the manga.

Domekano

Seems to be a decent adaption of the manga(note to expand on this once i read the manga and add that entry) really great story honestly, great romance drama, but you can sort of tell the story isn't over, will expand upon if there is another season.

Saekano

season 1 is great, season 2 is meh, anime about vn creation basically,.

Oregairu (S 2/3)

great anime, inspirtation to many, don't really have that much to say.

Dragon Ball GT

DBGT is a continuation of dragon ball (z if anime) by the anime team, it tries to be more akin to dragon balls roots instead of what it ended up being late game, ultimately ending with a mix of the styles, and its definitely is a must see

Bad Anime

bad anime is a section for anime i still want to list, but not necessarily recommend, it doesn't mean their bad, but in most cases theres a better form of the media, like a manga or visual novel.

Grisaia

i had a hard time putting this here because i love grisaia, but the anime makes it hard to process because of the story compression and speed, it's a good watch if you've read the VNs but i'd never recomend watching it first.

Island

same issues as the grisaia animes but even more chopped up and implementing foreshadowing that outright spoils the later parts.

Dragon Ball Z

okay time to piss a lot of people off, dragon ball z should be considered probably the worst manga to anime adaption of all time, the filler is everywhere, theres a ton of inconsistencies created by it, all of its boring, and you can tell the story was streched to stay behind the manga, there is actual good bits put in, to fix issues the manga had, but theres just too much nonsensical garbage to make it worth it, read the manga, if you have to watch it, skip the garlic jr arc, and use 2-3x speed on fights like the freeza fight.

Dragon Ball Super

continuation of dragon ball in anime form, this does not adapt the manga, toriyama writes notes and then toyotaro and toei fill in the blanks in different ways, many plot issues created by toei, plus the toriyama ones, plus some filler thats outright boring, honestly if you watch, watch the DBS movies and skip, the anime adaptions of the beerus and freeza arcs.

Key VNs (Air, Clannad, Kanon)

sort of the same issues as grisaia and island but less extreme, the route mergals, really kills them in a way, read the VNs first.

Manga

Note Vol means how far i read, if theres no volume number it means i've completed it or its not done in volumes.

Dragon Ball

a fantasy martial arts, adventure, sci fi, manga that ran for over 500 chapters, created by akira toriyama dragon ball goes from strong kids exploring the earth, to battles on a galactic scale, with actual emotional story telling, and simplestic but honest desires and philosphies you can probably learn more from this manga any school.

FullMetal Alchemist (Vol 12/27)

takes it self seriously in way other mangas can't, honestly probably one of the best, over the emotion moments, the manga basically has everything good about both animes, without the bad, its the definitive way to consume FMA.

Dragon Ball Super (Vol 13)

Great manga, continuation to the original dragon ball and minus toriyama's, lack of understanding of his own time travel system, is really thought out, and fleshed out, other than plot points entirely different than the anime, and a hundred times better.

Dragon Ball Multiverse

fan contiunation to the original dragonball, great manga, not as good as super but has clever usage of characters and their powers going for it.

Konosuba (Vol 10)

Konosuba is great ton of great jokes and general a lighthearted, but not unserious story, also some of the better written characters in manga.

One Piece (Vol 8)

pirate adventure manga, good writing and philosphies, fantastic.

Zelda "Legendary Edition" Manga

the legendary editon zelda mangas are great, they are all retelling of the games stories, within margin of error for story telling, some changes to fit the format, really helps flesh out the games and is definitely not a boring read.

Visual Novels

R(routes) is a measure of completion sadly it isn't as explainatory as vols or eps.

Island

a really complicated VN about someone who washes up on an inhabited island, honestly great story that i don't really know how to praise without spoiling.

The Fruit of Grisaia

a 130hour vn about the protagonist Kazami Yuuji going to a very special highschool, there are 5 heroines with 5 routes and 2 endings per route, the game has backstories for all the heroines and is extremely well written, this is another thing you can really learn a lot from.

The Labyrinth of Grisaia

the backstory of Kazami Yuuji and afterstories for the routes in The Fruit of Grisaia, would recommend just as much

The Eden of Grisaia

a story that ties together the plot threads of the previous Grisaia games, exists in a timeline where all the routes basically happened, can't recommend more.

Grisaia Side Games (Leisure, Afterglow, Melody

short 1 hour vns, all great, but ultimately not much.

Atri My Dear Moments

7-8 hour vn about a world with androids and where most of the world has sunken, inspiring and also really technical.

Kanon

classic VN not much for me to say about it as its less unique since people, have copied key's style, if you like clannad you'll like it.

Clannad

probably the most known VN of all times, some of the best writing, emotional highs, though. still a bit generic.

Tomoyo After

essentially an extension to clannad, a afterstory for tomoyo with different ends.

Air

Weakest of the key vns but not bad at all.
